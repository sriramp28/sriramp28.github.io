Modeling Motion, Heat, and Sound.

Physics-driven simulation for complex fluid, thermal, acoustic, structural, reacting, and geophysical systems. My work connects high-fidelity computation, finite-element and mesh-based multiphysics analysis, and transport diagnostics to understand how motion, heat, scalar mixing, materials constraints, and scale interactions control behaviour across natural and engineered systems.

Scientific and applied focus

Simulation for physical insight and consequential decisions.

The applications of my research span aerospace, energy, propulsion, thermal management, ocean mixing, environmental transport, process flows, and advanced manufacturing contexts where decisions depend on flow, heat, acoustics, scalar transport, instability, stress, vibration, and coupled multiphysics behaviour.

01

Fluid flow and turbulence

Analysis of shear layers, jets, separated flows, stratified and double-diffusive motion, coherent structures, transition, mixing, and turbulence organization using high-fidelity simulation, spectra, wavelets, topology, and event-based diagnostics.

02

Heat transfer and reacting systems

Quantification of thermal transport, scalar mixing, salt-fingering dynamics, chemical heat release, entropy production, and thermochemical coupling in flows where gradients and localized events dominate system behavior.

03

Acoustics and compressibility

Characterization of acoustic-compressive activity, wave-vortex interaction, broadband combustion noise, pressure-dilatation behavior, room and enclosure acoustics, wave propagation, and compressibility effects in high-speed and reacting flows.

04

Simulation workflows

Development of numerical workflows for model construction, finite-volume and finite-element discretization, meshing, parameter sweeps, HPC execution, filtering, nondimensionalization, quantitative diagnostics, visualization, and ParaView-ready inspection.

Applied simulation capability

Research outputs built from modeling, computation, and physical interpretation.

The core of my work combines governing-equation modeling, finite-element and finite-volume reasoning, algorithm development, high-performance execution, model-form assessment, transport analysis, unsteady signal processing, numerical diagnostics, and clear communication of dense simulation results for scientific interpretation, environmental and physical analysis, design, manufacturing, and education contexts.
